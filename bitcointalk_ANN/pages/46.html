SOG BETA TESTING

SEASON 2 ENDS, SEASON 3 STARTS SOON! ARE YOU READY?

As announced, with the end of February, the Beta Testing Season 2 is over, too.
During these 10 days, over 73 000 waves have been cleared and more than
430 cards evolved until the quad fused stage!

All players whose accounts were created before February 24th, and who have
entered their XCP address, were eligible to get rewards during the last season.
They received points according to their quad fused cards and then those points
were converted in BitCrystals, using the coefficient 10. Those have just been sent.

WHEN ONE THING ENDS, ANOTHER ONE BEGINS!
HERE'S THE SEASON 3!

All the non-blockchain cards, as well as the in-game currencies are being reset
and an UPDATED BETA VERSION is being released! What's new? Lot of items
from your "wish list" we have received through the feedback form or the slack
channel, are there:
  • We have the first version of a multiplayer! When choosing this option, you'll
    play against a random player's deck, completed with some other random
    cards.
  • Supplementary confirmation when crystallizing your cards has been added.
  • From now on, you have the possibility of crystallizing several cards at
    once    . By clicking on the "crystallize" button, situated on the Team screen,
    all your level-one unfused common cards will be crystallized.
  • Two brand new levels have been added.
  • The network usage has dramatically decreased, you can leave a level
    whenever you want ("exit" button), or you can switch the music off.
  • You can access the leaderboard directly from the main game screen (the
    map of Askian), as well as the beta-testing slack channel (by clicking on the
    "feedback" button), where you can meet our dev team.

SEASON 3 RULES
The Beta Testing Season 3 will start at the moment when all your offchain cards
and in-game currencies are reset. This will happen during this Thursday. We are
waiting for the iOS version approval and will reset it once everyone, no matter on
which platform, can access the new version. The Season 3 will last until March
24th. During this season, you have to clear as many waves as you can (as for the
first season), and there'll be a special bonus for those who end up in the top ten
at end the end of the season.

IMPORTANT: Please, remember that you have to fill in your Counterparty address!
Otherwise you can't receive your reward. If you haven't linked your SOG account to
your Counterparty address before the end of the season, you can't claim your reward
later.

If you any difficulties, our Gameplay&Cards Guide will help you!
