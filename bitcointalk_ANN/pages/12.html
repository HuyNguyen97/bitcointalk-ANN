Quote
I am wondering what will be the difference between BitCrystals and GameCredits? Are there a new advantages for gamers over GameCredits?

BitCrystals are counterparty assets pegged on the blockchain. It's like colored coin. BitCrystals are related to our product Spells of Genesis. Buying BitCrystals is like buying a fraction of the spells of Genesis. All game assed issued in spells of genesis are blockchain based and are purchasable with BitCrystals. From a gamer perspective since it's bitcoin blockchain based he can hold BTC BitCrystals and game asset on the same wallet. Success of BitCrystals is pegged on success of Spells of Genesis.
GameCredits are minable coin that use alternative blockchain (altcoin) their success is based on the success of this altchain over bitcoin.
