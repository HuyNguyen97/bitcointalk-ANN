SOG BETA TESTING - SEASON 2 REWARDS

As promised, we're bringing you more details about the Season 2 Rewards.

First of all, a huge thank you for your time spent on testing the Beta! It helps
us enormously to discover all the bugs and to progress towards the final
release!

For any "technical" terms used here, there's our brand new SoG Gameplay
and Cards Guide. Please, read it first, before asking any questions.

SEASON 2 REWARDS - RULES

  • For each "quad fused" card (see our guide for "quad fused" explanation), no
    matter at which level, you will receive a certain number of points (see the
    list below). These points will then be converted into BitCrystals.
  • Each of the cards listed below counts only once (i.e. even though you have
    6x Devastating Minotaur, it will count only once. Keep one Devastating Minotaur,
    crystallize the others and try to have for example a Royal Deer instead).
    We were forced to introduce this constraint due to the bug allowing multiple
    card fusions.
  • You will only have points for the cards being in your account at the end of
    the Season 2     (If you had a Devastating Minotaur at a certain moment this week,
    but you've crystallized it later, before the end of this season, you won't get any
    points for it).
  • Only accounts created before today, i.e. Wednesday, February 24th are
    eligible     to get points for the Season 2. The accounts created later will be eligible
    for the next season rewards.

QUAD FUSED CARDS&POINTS
  • Devastating Minotaur: 1 point
  • Royal Deer: 1 point
  • Weanara Assassin: 1 points
  • Viscula Mantis: 1 point
  • Centaur Archer: 3 points
  • Feodora, Princess of Spring: 3 points
  • Ice Unicorn: 3 points
  • Warrior Luminatis: 10 points
  • Warrior Obscuras: 10 points
  • Slashing Crirawin: 20 points
