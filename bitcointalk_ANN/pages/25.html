CARDS DISTRIBUTION AND STORES SYSTEM

Time has come to shed some light on the blockchain cards distribution and the system of SoG Stores.


SOG STORES
There are three types of Stores, all of them are now swapbot-based.
  • Partner Store
  • Premium Store
  • Public Store

PARTNER STORE
Access to the Partner Store is guaranteed for Token Sale contributors having reached the Partner rank. They have the
lifetime membership. In the future, it will be possible to reach the Partner rank temporary, the conditions will be set up
and announced in time.
During a transition period, while implementing the Partner Store on swapbot, until the second week of December, we will be
releasing one card a week for Partners only. Afterwards, there’ll be two cards a week and all new cards will systematically
be released at the Partner Store first. The cards offered at the Partner Store are available during a month. After this period,
cards from the Partner Store may go to the Premium Store, or to the Public one.

PREMIUM STORE
The Premium Store is accessible to the Token Sale contributors, from the Player Silver rank higher. As for the Partner Store,
it will be possible to get the access temporarily. Some cards will go from the Partner Store to the Premium Store and they’ll
never hit the Public Store. Premium Store hold specific cards that large public cannot access. You can identify the Premium
Store easily thanks to this icon, placed on the top of the swapbot.
New cards will come to the Premium Store one month after their release at the Partner Store. The first cards we will offer
you at the Premium Store will be the RIPPLECARD and the BLOCKSIZECD, on Tuesday, November 17th. The time and all details
will follow.



PUBLIC STORE
One card a week will be released at the Public Store. Until the beginning of December, these will be new cards, after the end of
the transition period, those will be cards issued previously at the Partner Store. You can identify the cards going from Partner
Store to the Public Store thanks to the icon below.




DISCOUNTS
As promised, several discounts will be applied to your purchases, according to your Partner/Player rank.

Discounts applied at the Partner and the Premium Store are
  • Partner Gold: 5% off
