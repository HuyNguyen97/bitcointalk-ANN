EverdreamSoft is now launching another from its series of limited editions of blockchain-based cards: SWARMCD.

ATTENTION: THE EVERDREAMSOFT’S ASSET MADE FOR SWARM IS CALLED SWARMCD, NOT SWARMCARD!

have lot of other projects onboard.

Spells of Genesis is our upcoming mobile game, which will integrate bitcoin and blockchain technology in its game economy as well as in its storyline. It’s targeted for

Discover "Clef, Warden of Wanders"




CLEFCARD

EverdreamSoft, in collaboration with Clef, who is providing a secure two-factor authentification system, is launching the CLEFCARD, a blockchain-based asset,
playable as “Warden of Wonders” in EverdreamSoft's Moonga and Spells of Genesis, or freely tradable on the blockchain.


Only 1000 units of CLEFCARD will be released, the 1st card is purchasable for an equivalent of 3.50 USD, then the price will gradually increase with the number
of cards being purchased. This means that the sooner you purchase your CLEFCARD, the less you’ll pay. In the future we expect the cards to acquire more
value as they are collector items.

BUILD YOUR COLLECTION

After FDCARD, SJCXCARD, GEMZCARD and recently XCPCARD, SWARMCD and  ETHEREUMCARD you can now broaden your collection with CLEFCARD.
To be sure your collection is complete, check all our blockchain-based trading cards HERE.
All EverdreamSoft’s special blockchain-based cards are playable in Moonga and SoG, as well as freely tradable outside the games. When you purchase one of our
blockchain-based cards, you will own your game asset not only within the game, but also on the blockchain.

ABOUT US

EverdreamSoft is a swiss based company specialized in the field of mobile games development. With Moonga, our main trading card game, we were among the
first to have a free-to-play game on the AppStore in 2009. Since then, the genre has evolved in unexpected ways.

Spells of Genesis is our upcoming mobile game, which will integrate bitcoin and blockchain technology in its game economy as well as in its storyline. It’s
targeted for release later this year.

The game launch is preceded by the BitCrystals Token Sale, a crowdfund which will allow us to raise funds necessary to the full game development. The BitCrystal
Token Sale will start on July 29th, 2015.
